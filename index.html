about us

The ‘#GRAB’ name has been developed from the idea around a fast paced , quick concept , where everything is prepared and ready to be enjoyed. The yellow color represents happiness and energy which are attriutes for the brand #grab.

We have designed #grab to have geometric timber paneling and square tilling to the real wall in white and contrasting charcoal grey. The design has been enhanced by using a pop of yellow, which is a color the creates a positive and happy atmosphere.

#grab menu has been developed fit for fast environment, quick grabs to go include ready to pick fresh juices, sandwiches, snacks and salads.

Established in 2015 in Bahrain, at AlRayah Catering we offer complete catering sublime culinary experiences across a spectrum of cuisines that tickle the senses and awe the tastebuds, for your private, corporate events & industrial catering needs.

Backed by this legacy, #grab was introduced as a modern and covenient option for meals on-the-go. A carefully curated menu brings flavors from around the world served in bespoke packaging for fuss-free dining. The brand quickly expanded across Bahrain and now operates multiple outlets around the Kingdom including independant kiosks, instituitional catering and premium grocery store.
